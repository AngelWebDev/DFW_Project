We’d Like To Welcome You To Our Journey

Over the past 6 years, our company, Four Twelve Roofing, has been building something special here in Baltimore. It started with purchasing a single vacant homeand now we are deeply intertwined in our community. A local construction company specializing in Roofing and Restoration, we love to be a part of quality construction projects that improve the city of Baltimore.

We are always looking at new and exciting projects that we hope will continue to make positive impacts. Our management team has over 20 years of combined construction and project management experience. Four Twelve aims to build quality homes and manage its relationships from a place of honesty and integrity. We are building something great here in our beloved Baltimore, and we invite all who appreciate our work to be a part of it.

Mission Statement

At Four Twelve Roofing, quality is EVERYTHING. We take pride in all that we do, whether it be laying a row of shingles or sweeping up your driveway at the end of the workday. In every project we take on – we do it once, do it right and provide our clients unparalleled customer service at every step of the way. Our goal is to honor our clientsand create relationships that last a lifetime.

We have been in your shoes. We started as homebuilders and were disappointed with the quality of roofing companies in Baltimore. We had a dream to provide something better to our friends, family and everyone else in Baltimore. And that is how Four Twelve Roofing was born. Choose Four Twelve Roofing and you’ll notice the difference from the moment we answer your call. We are here to serve you in any and all of your roofing needs.
